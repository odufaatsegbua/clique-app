Our Guidelines

It is forbidden to: Write a profile or trend piece about the clique community without explicit permission from someone on the Clique team. Quote from a conversation within the Girlboss community, screen capture ANY content (files, threads, comments) from the Girlboss community and share or publicize in any way, or in any other way compromise the confidentiality of the members of this group.
