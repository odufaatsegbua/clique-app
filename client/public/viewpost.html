Struggling with anxiety

by MelissaJohnston

Posted on January 5th, 2020 at 12:00 PM

I never noticed how bad my anxiety was until I noticed I was constantly biting and chewing my lip as well as always grinding my teeth and clenching my jaw. What can I do about this?

Normally this happens during times of stress like with school or family things but I've noticed I also do it when I watch intense movies or find myself in uncomfortable situations.

I was wondering if anyone else struggles with this and therefore has any recommendations on how I should deal with it. Possibly any therapists?
