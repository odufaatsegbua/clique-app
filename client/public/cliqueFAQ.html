Discover a community for you

What is the Girlboss professional network? Girlboss is a platform built for ambitious women. It’s a place for entrepreneurs, career pros, side-hustlers, freelancers, and gig-economy workers to ask questions, exchange ideas, and connect with each other. We’re creating a space for you to share not just what you do, but who you are. Who is allowed on the platform? Anyone who is looking for the tools and resources to succeed in both their personal and professional lives. What does the free membership include? Access to a like-minded community that is here for you, including fellow peers, future mentors, and more, so you can ask the work, money, and business questions that can’t be answered on Google. A gorgeous profile that isn’t just your resume, it’s your story. The ability to message thousands of ambitious women across the globe. Weekly Digital Firesides with today’s thought leaders. Our video library, which includes 65+ hours of past Girlboss Rally conferences and career resources. What should I post? No topic is off limits here. The Girlboss platform is a place where you can ask and answer questions about all things life, career, and money, and share your vulnerabilities in a safe environment. Read more about our community guidelines below. How do I send a message to another member? To get started, send a connection request to the community member you’d like to chat with by clicking into they profile and clicking on the button that says ‘Say Hey’. How do I get in touch with the Girlboss team? If you have any questions, thoughts, or suggestions, please get in touch at community@girlboss.com. Community Guidelines: This place is built on Girlboss’s own internal value system—this is important because it’s what we stand for and commit to both internally and externally: Humor: We take our work seriously, but we don’t take ourselves seriously. Resourcefulness: We do more with less, but raise our hands when we need help. Vulnerability: We have real conversations about where we are and what we need to succeed—and are comfortable with change. Curiosity: We are eternal learners, ask dumb questions (but never treat others’ ideas or Q’s as dumb) and are comfortable trying and failing. Inclusivity: We value diversity of age, race, gender, religion, ability, experience, class, size, and more. Here’s the essential takeaway: Always show respect to others so that we can continue to enjoy good vibes in the Girlboss community. More details here. What is the privacy policy? You can see the privacy policy here. How do I report a post that goes against the community guidelines? Click into the post, then click on the grey flag in the bottom right hand corner of the post. How do I report a response that goes against the community guidelines? Click on the grey flag in the bottom right hand corner of the response. How do I report a member that goes against the community guidelines? Click on the member’s profile, then click on the three dots on the right hand side, and then click on the ‘Report User’. How do I reset my password? First, make sure you are logged out of the Girlboss platform. Then, on the login page, you will see a button that says ‘Forgot Password’, from there you will be able to submit your email address to get a password reset email sent straight to your inbox. How do I change my password? Click on your profile photo in the top right hand corner, then click into your settings to change your password. How do I edit my profile? Click on your profile photo in the top right hand corner, then click on the ‘View My Profile’ button, and then click on ‘Edit Profile’. How do I delete a post? Click into the post, then click on the three dots in the on the right hand side, and then click on ‘Delete Post’. How do I edit a post? Click into the post, then click on the three dots in the on the right hand side, and then click on ‘Edit Post’. How do I delete a response? Click on the three dots on the right hand side of your response, and then click on ‘Delete Reply’. How do I edit a response? Click on the three dots on the right hand side of your response, and then click on ‘Edit Reply’. How do I change my username? You can’t change your username on the Girlboss platform, so choose your username wisely. How do I delete my account? Please send an email to help@girlboss.com using the email that is associated with your account and we can delete your account.
