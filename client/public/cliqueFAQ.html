Frequently Asked Questions

What is the CLIQUE community?
Clique is an online platform built for ALL women. It’s a place for everyone to share stories and connect with each other. We’re creating a space for you to share not just what you are struggling with, but who you are.
Who is allowed on the platform?
Anyone who is looking for the tools and resources to succeed in both their personal and professional lives.
What does the free membership include?
Access to a like-minded community that is here for you, including physchologists, therapists and counselors. Your own profile that speaks your personal story. The ability to message thousands of women across the globe. Access to our podcast.
What should I post?
No topic is off limits here. The Clique platform is a place where you can ask and answer questions about all things life, career, and money, and share your vulnerabilities in a safe environment. Read more about our community guidelines below.
How do I send a message to another member?
To get started, send a connection request to the community member you’d like to chat with by clicking into they profile and clicking on the button that says ‘Say Hey’.
How do I get in touch with the Clique team?
If you have any questions, thoughts, or suggestions, please get in touch at community@clique.com.
What is the privacy policy?
You can see the privacy policy here.
How do I report a member that goes against the community guidelines?
Click on the member’s profile, then click on the three dots on the right hand side, and then click on the ‘Report User’.
How do I delete a post?
Click into the post, then click on the three dots in the on the right hand side, and then click on ‘Delete Post’.
How do I edit a post?
Click into the post, then click on the three dots in the on the right hand side, and then click on ‘Edit Post’.
How do I delete a response?
Click on the three dots on the right hand side of your response, and then click on ‘Delete Reply’.
How do I edit a response?
Click on the three dots on the right hand side of your response, and then click on ‘Edit Reply’.
How do I change my username?
You can’t change your username on the Girlboss platform, so choose your username wisely.
How do I delete my account?
Please send an email to help@gclique.com using the email that is associated with your account and we can delete your account.
